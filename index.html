Present Simple

Rule:

Use Present Simple for usual things and facts, habbits, live status (student, teacher, etc.)


Markers: usually, always, often, every day/week/months/years, once a day/week/etc.


How to use:

to be
+ S+am/is/are
- S+am/is/are+not
? Am/is/are+S

Examples:

  1. I am a student
  2. He is a teacher
  3. We are workers

  4. I am not a gamer
  5. We are not a family
  6. He is not my father

  7. Is he a student?
  8. Are we an animal?
  9. Am I a mother?

other verbs
+ S+V/Vs/Ves
- S+do/does not+V
? Do/Does+S+V

Examples:

  1. I like cheese balls
  2. They go to the swimminl pool every week
  3. She drinks water every hour

  4. I don't go to the cinema every month
  5. We don't see anything
  6. He doesn't love me anymore

  7. Do you go to the overseas every year?
  8. Does she like him?
  9. Do you take a shower every day?

Past Simple

Rule:

Use Past simple when you want to tell about something in the Past


Markers: yesterday,a day/week/month ago, last week, in December (any month)


How to use:

to be
+ S+was/were
- S+was/were+not
? Was/were+S

Examples:

  1. I was here yesterday
  2. She wasn't eat yesterday
  3. Where were we?

Future Simple

Rule:

Use Future Simple when u want to say about your opinion, guesses, facts about future or if you make decision right now


How to use:

to be
+ S+will+V
- S+will+not+V
? Will+S+V

Examples:

  1. We will think about your decision
  2. I think I won't be here
  3. Will you guess how many years this animal live?
